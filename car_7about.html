XC90

Specifications-

The Volvo XC90 is a mid-size luxury SUV manufactured and marketed by Volvo Cars since 2002 and now in its second generation. The first generation was introduced at the 2002 North American International Auto Show and used the Volvo P2 platform shared with the first generation Volvo S80 and other large Volvo cars.

Volvo Car India has launched the updated XC90 SUV priced at Rs 89.90 lakh (ex-showroom). The 2021 XC90 now gets a host of upgrades, most notably the new 2.0-litre mild-hybrid petrol engine replacing the diesel motor. This is the same engine with a 48-volt electric motor that was introduced on the XC60 and S90 recently.
